Snack Hacks is the world's premier snackathon and the first of its kind. The goal is simple: snack as much as humanly possible for 36 hours. This scrumptious celebration will bring together 1,000 students from all over the world who just can't stop eating. With all-star judges such as Gordon Ramsay and a veritable smorgasboard of greasy sponsors Snack Hacks will be truly memorable.
