About the Event

Hack the North is Canada's premier Hackathon.

Hack the North is a 36 hour event where 1,000 students of different skill levels come together, form teams, and create a unique software or hardware project from scratch. With industry leaders, world class mentors, great food and a ton of hardware resources - you're in for an amazing time!
