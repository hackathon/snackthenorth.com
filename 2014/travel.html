Travel Guidelines

Schedule your travel for Hack The North such that you aim to arrive in Toronto (Pearson Airport) by 7 PM or Waterloo by 8:30 PM on Friday (Sept 19), and leave no earlier than 7 PM (4-4:30 PM from Waterloo campus - Event ends at 3:30 PM) on Sunday (Sept 21). Please note the following rules for reimbursement:

We are only reimbursing the cheapest form of transport to Toronto/Waterloo that we’ve deemed convenient. We've used Kayak and Google Flights for our quotes

Reimbursement Chart

If you are coming from outside of Canada, please make sure you have a valid passport!!!!

  • You will be reimbursed for the travel costs after the event
  • If you’re in highschool, reference the university/college closest to you.
  • If your transportation requires advanced purchase, it must be purchased by/on August 19th. If you don’t book by then, your transportation will not be reimbursed.
  • If your transportation does not require advanced purchase, you must tell us how you will be getting to Waterloo by August 19th as well.
  • Once you have made your booking, we will not cover any change fees.
  • If we are sending a bus to your campus, your reimbursement is $0
  • We will not cover any unnecessary travel fees, such as an upgrades, food, etc.
  • If your ticket is purchased by someone else, please let us know of this via email.
  • Do not count on us to cover the cost of transportation to/from the YYZ (Toronto Airport) [see next point] nor to/from the airport in your home area. We also do not cover any fees that may come from your travel.
  • We will be providing shuttle services to and from our campus to Toronto Pearson Airport on Friday and Sunday. If you miss the shuttles (there will be a few) then the rule above applies (no cost coverage).
  • If possible, please consider traveling with a car with a group of fellow participants. We will cover the cost of gas (20 cents/km driven) and the cost of rental for a car. There is parking on campus, though it’s somewhat limited, so make sure to reach out to us so we can direct you to the best places.
  • Once you have made your purchase, please upload all receipts to the website (please make sure this receipt shows how much was paid and that you actually made the purchase).
  • If you research transportation from your location and find the travel cap to be unreasonable, email us at hello@hackthenorth.com.

Note that if you book tickets, but then do not show up to Hack the North, you will not be reimbursed.
